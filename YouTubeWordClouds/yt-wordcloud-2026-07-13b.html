How This Works

Extraction. Claude drives a real Chrome tab to my YouTube homepage, scrolls the feed, and reads every video card straight out of the DOM. No API, no watch history — just what YouTube chose to show me that day.

Word count. Titles are tokenized, stopwords dropped, and the 130 most frequent words drawn with wordcloud2.js — a word’s size is its frequency.

Color. Words are scored against the NRC Emotion Lexicon (Mohammad & Turney, 2013) into Plutchik’s eight basic emotions, with hues from Cymbolism’s word–color data. Neutral words stay slate. The bar above is the day’s emotion signature.
